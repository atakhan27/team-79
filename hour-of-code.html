Preparation for the Hour of Code

To organize our Hour of Code event, we collaborated with Dunya IB School to bring the joy of coding to an MYP1 class (5th grade). After careful planning, we coordinated with class mentors, informed parents, and prepared ourselves to make this session engaging and impactful.

The Event

During the Hour of Code, students participated in interactive coding challenges, like guiding characters in games on Code.org. The room buzzed with excitement as students collaborated, learned new skills, and solved fun puzzles.

  • Activity 1: Guiding Angry Birds to catch the pig
  • Activity 2: Helping Plants vs. Zombies achieve their goals
  • Learning Outcomes: Basic programming concepts like loops, functions, and debugging

Achievements

Our students displayed remarkable adaptability and problem-solving skills. From solving challenging puzzles to creating animations, they showcased their ability to think algorithmically and work collaboratively.

Challenges

While the event was a success, some challenges were faced along the way:

  • Understanding loops and their effects caused confusion for some students.
  • Combining multiple actions into functions was difficult but overcame with guidance.
  • Limited time posed a challenge, but teamwork ensured every student received assistance.

Special Thanks

We extend our gratitude to the mentors and parents of Dunya IB School for their support and assistance in organizing this event. Their contributions were invaluable in making the Hour of Code a success.
